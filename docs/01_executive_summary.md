# Executive Summary

## Project

Environmental Operational Business Intelligence Warehouse

---

## Definition

The Environmental Operational Business Intelligence Warehouse is a custom-designed operational data warehouse that consolidates business data from multiple Microsoft Excel workbooks into a structured SQL database for reporting, operational analysis, and future business intelligence.

The warehouse organizes operational data into staging, dimension, and fact tables to create a centralized, consistent source of truth for the company's core business processes.

---

## Business Problem

The company's operational data existed across multiple independent Excel workbooks maintained for payroll, laboratory reporting, work authorizations, contracts, billing, and payment tracking.

Although each workbook solved a specific business need, the data was fragmented, duplicated, and difficult to analyze across departments.

The warehouse centralizes these data sources into a single relational database that supports consistent reporting and simplifies future analytics.

---

## Project Outcome

Version 1 of the warehouse includes:

- Operational staging layer
- Dimension layer
- Fact layer
- SQL ETL processes
- Data dictionary
- Grain documentation
- Relational database model
- Repository documentation

The completed warehouse serves as the foundation for future dashboards, KPI reporting, and business intelligence applications.
