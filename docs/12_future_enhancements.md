# Future Enhancements

## Why This Chapter Matters

Version 1.0 focuses on building a well-documented operational data warehouse.

This chapter identifies potential enhancements that may be considered after Version 1.0 has been completed.

These ideas are intentionally outside the current project scope.

---

# Future Reporting

Potential reporting enhancements include:

- Interactive dashboards.
- KPI scorecards.
- Executive reporting.
- Operational performance metrics.

---

# ETL Enhancements

Potential ETL improvements include:

- Automated data imports.
- Scheduled ETL processes.
- Additional validation checks.
- Improved error logging.

---

# Warehouse Enhancements

Possible warehouse enhancements include:

- Additional fact tables.
- Expanded dimension tables.
- Historical tracking where appropriate.
- Additional bridge and mapping tables.

---

# Analytics

Potential analytical enhancements include:

- Trend analysis.
- Productivity metrics.
- Financial analysis.
- Operational performance reporting.

---

# Technology

Future versions may expand the technical implementation through tools such as:

- Python
- Tableau
- Power BI
- Automated reporting solutions

The warehouse has been designed to support future growth without changing the underlying business model.

---

# Version 1.0

Version 1.0 represents the foundation of the Environmental Operational Business Intelligence Warehouse.

The current priority is completing, validating, and documenting the warehouse before expanding its capabilities.

Future ideas are maintained separately in:

- `docs/version_2_ideas.md`

This allows Version 1.0 to remain focused on delivering a complete and well-documented operational warehouse before additional features are introduced.
