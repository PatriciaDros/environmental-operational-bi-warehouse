# Environmental Operational Business Intelligence Warehouse

**Version 1.0**

---

## A Custom Operational Data Warehouse

A custom-designed operational data warehouse that consolidates multiple Microsoft Excel-based business processes into a centralized SQL reporting database for an environmental consulting company.

---

## Author

Patricia D.

---

## Purpose

This repository documents the design and implementation of Version 1.0 of the Environmental Operational Business Intelligence Warehouse.

The project demonstrates the complete lifecycle of designing and implementing a custom operational data warehouse, from identifying real-world business problems through database design, ETL development, documentation, and validation.
